Chinmay Savadikar

I am a Machine Learning Engineer with expertise in Computer Vision. I like to develop robust, end-to-end Computer Vision solutions using both, Deep Learning and traditional Computer Vision algorithms.

I have extensive experience with problems in Image Recognition, Object Detection, and Semantic Segmentation, and like to work on the full stack of Machine Learning and Deep Learning pipeline - Data Visualization, Data Annotation, incremental training and fine-tuning of models, and deployment.

I graduated from Savitribai Phule Pune University (you may know it as University of Pune) with a Bachelor's degree in Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering. I work as a Software Engineer at Persistent Systems Ltd.

On the non-technical side, I like reading fiction and non-fiction (you can take a look at my Goodreads profile , if you'd like to), although I took a looong hiatus before resuming the habit. I enjoy playing Football, listening to Oldies Rock Music, and watching an occasional sitcom.
