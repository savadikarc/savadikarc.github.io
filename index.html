I am a first year Ph.D. student at North Carolina State University. My research interest lies in Continual Learning, with broader interest in efficient and robust learning for visual tasks.

I am also interested in applied research and software engineering, and worked with the Precision Sustainable Agriculture initiative at NC State prior to starting my Ph.D. to build Computer Vision and Software solutions for problems in agriculture.

Prior to joining NC State, I worked as a Machine Learning Engineer at Persistent Systems, where I worked on Deep Learning for Medical Imaging. I also spent some time developing internal SDKs for the Data Science team, and setting up MLOps frameworks.

Outside of research, I like reading fiction and non-fiction. I also enjoy playing Football, listening to Oldies Rock Music, and watching sitcoms.
