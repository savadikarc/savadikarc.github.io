I am a Master's student at North Carolina State University. I am interested in Computer Vision and Machine Learning, more specifically in robust and efficient visual learning.

I am also interested in applied research and software engineering, and work with the Precision Sustainable Agriculture initiative at NC State to build Computer Vision and Software solutions for problems in agriculture.

Prior to joining NC State, I worked as a Machine Learning Engineer at Persistent Systems, where I worked on Deep Learning for Medical Imaging. I also spent some time developing internal SDKs for the Data Science team, and setting up MLOps frameworks.

I graduated from University of Pune with a Bachelor's degree in Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering, where I split my time between the Robotics and the Football (I will not call it Soccer) teams.

I like reading fiction and non-fiction. I also enjoy playing Football, listening to Oldies Rock Music, and watching an occasional sitcom.

You can contact me through my email, on Twitter, or on LinkedIn.
